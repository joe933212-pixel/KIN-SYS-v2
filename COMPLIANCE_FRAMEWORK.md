# Compliance Framework

## Regulatory Requirements

1. **Data Protection Regulation** (GDPR, CCPA)
   - Requirement to encrypt personal data.
   - Right to access and delete personal data.

2. **Financial Regulations** (SOX, PCI-DSS)
   - Ensure accurate financial reporting.
   - Maintain security of payment data.

3. **Health Regulations** (HIPAA)
   - Protect patient health information.
   - Ensure data privacy and security.

## Integration Points

- **API Integrations**: Ensure APIs adhere to compliance standards.
- **Data Storage**: Utilize compliant cloud storage providers.
- **User Access Controls**: Implement role-based access aligned with regulations.
